About


The Spite Level Editor is a public level editor for ULTRAKILL. Where you can upload your level into Envy Level Loader at any time, no matter the quality.

In this wiki you will learn how to create levels, from start to finish. It is important to note that even if this wiki is for Spite LE, information given here can also be applied to the Rude Level Editor, however, Rude LE has some differences.
